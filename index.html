How ResiFund Works

A simple, transparent process from investment to impact

01

Project Selection

Communities and NGOs submit climate vulnerability data. Our AI-powered Resilience Viability Engine scores projects based on climate risk, equity, and SDG alignment.

02

Asset Tokenization

Approved infrastructure is tokenized into ERC-20 tokens on Polygon blockchain. Investors can purchase fractional ownership starting at $10.

03

Deployment & Monitoring

Verified partners deploy climate kits with IoT sensors and QR tracking. Real-time data feeds into live impact dashboards.

04

Impact & Returns

Track measurable climate impact, community feedback, and financial returns. Continuous learning improves future projects.
